Hula Hooper

Cirque style hula hooper spins multiple hoops (up to 8) with option for LED lighted hoops at no additional charge. Performance can be ambient (1 or 2 hoops) traveling through the crowd or on stage. Additionally, hula hooper can perform a choreographed act with 8 hoops - approximately 6.5 minute long (requires a 12 x 15 ft stage or cleared area).
