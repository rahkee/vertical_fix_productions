About Us

Vertical Fix creates exciting experiences on a national level from onstage performances to interactive environments.  Impress your guests or inspire your employees with a one-of-a-kind event experience, tailored to a theme or
concept of your choice.

Our skilled performers and entertainers have years of experience with aerial artistry and circus entertainment.  Whether you are looking for a single aerialist, group performance, ambient aerial or an entire tailor-made
aerial show, Vertical Fix is the perfect choice for your upcoming event!
